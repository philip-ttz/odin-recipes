Pasta

Description

Pasta (Italian for dough) is the name given in Italian cuisine to pasta in various sizes, types and shapes.

In this recipe we will going to be making tagliatelle.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl till smooth.
  2. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Kneed the dough and add water if needed.
  4. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Spread it out using a pastaholy till 2mm thick.
  6. Cut it in stripes and boil in hot water.
