Burger Bun

Description

Ingredients

  • 300 ml Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 45g Butter
  • 1El Sugar
  • 21g Yeast
  • 500g Flour
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Mix milk, sugar, yeast and butter and let it rest for a little.
  2. Mix flour with a pinch of salt and add the yeast mixture as well as one egg.
  3. Kneed the dough.
  4. Let it rest for 60 minutes.
  5. Divide the dough into 8 pieces and form out the buns.
  6. Let it rest for another 45 minutes.
  7. Mix the other egg with a little milk and cover the buns.
  8. Bake it in the oven at 190 degrees celsius for 20 minutes till golden brown.
